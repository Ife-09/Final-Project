Thailand

Thailand had the world’s longest-reigning monarch. The revered King Bhumibol Adulyadej the Great reigned over Thailand for 70 years and 126 days making him the world’s longest-reigning head of state and the longest-reigning monarch in Thai history.
Thailand proudly houses the world’s largest solid gold Buddha statue, weighing a staggering 5.5 tons, tucked away in the Wat Traimit temple in Bangkok.
